# Power of AI — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name
**Power of AI**
Website: powerofai.nl

## Mission
Wij helpen Nederlandse bedrijven om de kracht van AI te benutten — van strategie tot implementatie.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Electric Blue**: #2563EB (primary actions, links)
- **Cyan Accent**: #06B6D4 (highlights, gradients)
- **Deep Indigo**: #4F46E5 (secondary accent)

### Neutral Colors
- **White**: #FFFFFF (primary background)
- **Snow**: #F8FAFC (section backgrounds, alternating)
- **Light Gray**: #F1F5F9 (cards, subtle backgrounds)
- **Slate 400**: #94A3B8 (secondary text)
- **Slate 700**: #334155 (body text)
- **Slate 900**: #0F172A (headings)

### Gradient
- **Primary Gradient**: linear-gradient(135deg, #2563EB, #06B6D4)
- **Accent Gradient**: linear-gradient(135deg, #4F46E5, #2563EB, #06B6D4)
- **Subtle Glow**: radial-gradient(circle, rgba(37,99,235,0.15), transparent)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter (700, 800 weight) — clean, modern, professional
- **Body**: Inter (400, 500 weight)
- **Accent/Special**: Space Grotesk (for numbers, stats, tech feel)

## Design Principles
1. **Light & Airy** — white/light backgrounds, lots of whitespace
2. **Bold Accents** — vibrant blue/cyan gradients for CTAs and highlights
3. **Subtle Depth** — soft shadows, glassmorphism cards, hover effects
4. **Motion** — smooth scroll animations, subtle transitions, floating elements
5. **Trust** — clean layout, professional imagery, social proof

## Logo
- Primary: "Power of AI" text mark with lightning bolt icon integrated into "AI"
- Colors: Use primary gradient on logo
- Ensure sufficient contrast on both light and dark backgrounds

## Voice & Tone
- Professional but accessible
- Dutch language, clear and direct
- Avoid jargon — explain AI concepts simply
- Confident and forward-thinking
